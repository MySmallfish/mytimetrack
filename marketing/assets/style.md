# Visual Style Notes (For Banners/Pages)

## Direction
"Time grid" meets "clean invoice".
- Minimal, high-contrast, with a subtle grid pattern.
- Bright accent colors that feel energetic, not corporate.

## Palette
- Ink: `#0B0F14`
- Paper: `#F7F2E8`
- Accent (brand): `#409E99` (from `Color(red: 0.25, green: 0.62, blue: 0.60)`)
- Amber: `#FFB347`
- Slate: `#5B6775`

## Typography (suggested)
- Headings: Space Grotesk / Fraunces (bold)
- Body: IBM Plex Sans / Source Sans 3
- Mono accents: Space Mono

## Imagery
- Use the day timeline screenshot as the hero.
- Keep screenshots inside a simple "phone" frame for social cards.
