Pricing FAQ

Do I need to create an account?

No. MyTimetrack works without an account.

Is there a subscription later?

No subscription. The app is priced as a one-time lifetime purchase.

Can I re-download on a new phone?

Yes. Your App Store / Google Play account manages purchases and re-downloads (use the same store account).

Do you store my data on a server?

MyTimetrack is designed to feel local-first. You export/share reports when you choose to.
